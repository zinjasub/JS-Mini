You can play versus a friend or KIT™

The object of Connect 4™ is to connect four of the same colored pieces in a line (either red or yellow) in horizontal, vertical or diagnol.
You have the option to play against our AI, KIT™, or a friend.
If you choose to play against a friend, flip a coin to see who goes first.
Heads is red and tails is yellow.
If you choose to place against KIT™ you are the red player.
